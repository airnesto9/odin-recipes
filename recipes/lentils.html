Nesto's best dish for busy boys

Easy and low effort one pot pasta, with "alla genovese", tomatoes and mozzarella!

Ingriedients

  • 500g Linguine
  • 75g Pesto alla genovese
  • 150g tomatoes
  • 125g Mozzarella
  • pepper & salt

Steps

  1. Heatup water for the noodles
  2. Cut the mozzarella and tomatoes into bite-sized pieces
  3. When the water reached its boiling point, put the noodles in the pot
  4. Boil the linguine to your preffered density
  5. Empty the water from the pot and mix everything in there
